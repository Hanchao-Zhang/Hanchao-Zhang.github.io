Liver damage, gastrointestinal symptoms and severity of disease in COVID-19

Liver and GI symptoms lead to prolonged disease progression and higher mortality rates in older COVID-19 patients with a history of smoking. These symptoms may be overlooked during the course of illness but appear to be more insidious than respiratory symptoms in COVID-19 patients.
Hong-Gang Ren, Luqman Safdar, Keven Blighe, Nan Huo, Hanchao Zhang, Lanqiu Yao, etc
